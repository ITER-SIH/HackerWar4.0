"About Codex"
We here at codex are identified as coders only because we know what it takes to start from scratch and keep on scratching till we achieve our goals. We have seen our aluminis sharpen their skills, work in teams, brainstorm ideas and get placed in fortune companies. Our goal is not to teach anyone rather, learn together.

"About Event"
This is the 4th edition of the HackerWar contest series and is only for students currently enrolled in ITER, SOADU. This hackathon will serve as the selection ground for nominating teams to participate in Smart India Hackathon 2023 (SIH 2023).

"About SIH"
Smart India Hackathon 2023 is a nationwide initiative to provide students a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving.

"Registration Guidelines"
1. Each team would mandatorily comprise of 6 members should be from ITER, SOA only including the team leader and at least one female team member
2. Every participant should join Discord (mandatory). Link
3. The team leader must take consent from all team members before filling out the Google form as team composition changes will not be permitted once established.

"Hackathon Guidelines"
1. Every team needs to push the codes at an interval of every 2 hours until the event lasts. from its commencement.
2. During the hackathon, no participant should completely drop off the server but can take a temporary break; they need to get permission from the faculty coordinator.
3. At least two members should always be active in the Discord VCs, and at the time of the evaluation, every member of the team should be present.
4. All work related to the project must be developed or coded during the event only. It is not permitted to use the pre-built project during the event.
